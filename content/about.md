# About
**Art Research Productions** is a film and audiovisual production company created in 2011 by Alec T. Aguletti. 

It is based in Sinaia, in the Romanian Carpathians, and aims to develop audiovisual projects for international markets. To build its productions, **ARP** uses dramaturgy but also, among other things, communication and information sciences, systems and street art. 

In general, **Art Research** likes to anchor itself in reality to transfigure it with all the more brilliance!
